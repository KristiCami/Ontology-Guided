\section{RESULTS}
\subsection{Ontology Element Extraction}
Table~\ref{tab:table1} reports the precision, recall, and F1--score for ontology element extraction across baselines (exact triple matching). The LLM--only baseline is averaged over two seeds. While the symbolic component achieves the highest recall, the full neuro--symbolic pipeline yields a stronger precision--recall balance than either baseline.

\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\caption{ONTOLOGY ELEMENT EXTRACTION QUALITY}
\label{tab:table1}
\begin{tabular}{lccc}
\toprule
Method & Precision & Recall & F1 \\
\midrule
LLM-only (avg.\ 2 seeds) & 0.260 & 0.336 & 0.292 \\
Symbolic-only & 0.275 & 0.552 & 0.367 \\
Ours (no repair) & 0.158 & 0.552 & 0.245 \\
Ours (full) & 0.213 & 0.536 & 0.305 \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

This indicates that the repair loop primarily targets semantic quality (e.g., competency questions) rather than resolving SHACL errors in this run.
