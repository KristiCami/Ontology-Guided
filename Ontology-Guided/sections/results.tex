\subsection{Domain Adaptation}
When switching from ATM to healthcare and automotive datasets, the pipeline maintains high precision and recall with only minor degradation. This demonstrates that domain porta\-bility can be achieved by swapping ontologies and SHACL shapes without retraining the neural component.
